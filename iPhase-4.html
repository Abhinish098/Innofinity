ACCIDENT DETECTION

Tracking being the pivot of our app, we have incorporated some easy features which is just one click away.



Windows opening during Accident


In the rare event of a sinking vehicle or smoke due to accident, windows of the vehicle will automatically open for ventilation and escape.



Driver Sleep Detection


An AI based system trained to perfectness, our device alerts the driver when it detects drowsiness or inattention,wakes him up to prevent accidents.

Shock Detection


Considering all the possible extent at which a car can take a shock preventing false alarm a system is setup which will immediately inform police station and nearby hospitals so they can reach as soon as possible.

Fire or Ignition detection


Fire detection is one of the most crucial aspects of an accident, hence our device comes with an in-built fire detection system which can differentiate between cigarette smoke and a fire. On detecting a fire, it informs the nearby fire stations and alerts the driver of the car via SMS.

SOS Features:


During the course of accident, this feature would redirect the number to the closest health centre and 5 contacts saved by the owner, sending messages for emergency action along with the location of the accident.
