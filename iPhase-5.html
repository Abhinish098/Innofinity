VEHICLE CONDITION MONITORING

In order to prevent any unauthorized access and break -in, our devices are designed with sensors that would notify our customers promptly in the adverse situation of suspicious activity.

Fuel Sensor Alerts:


The amount of fuel present in the fuel tank is determined by the fuel level sensor and accordingly the SMS is sent to the owner of the car via GSM module. Our app sends an alert message to the owner if the fuel level is low. If the driver of the vehicle fills fuel in the tank then an alert message is sent to the owner and the message contains location of the place, amount of fuel the driver fills. It thus helps the user to keep a record of fuel consumption thereby monitoring the vehicle engine condition.
